About Tarrant County Pest Control

Tarrant County Pest Control is a professional pest and termite control company located in Grand Prairie, Texas serving the Dallas/Fort Worth area. Serving all the cities in Tarrant County and some cities in Dallas County.

Brent Russell started Tarrant County Pest Control in 1997 and does every pest and termite job himself. You just can't get that kind of service from larger pest control companies.

"I think people like doing business with an owner that takes pride in his company and works hard to earn their repeat and referral business. I try to be professional, dependable, and affordable."

Tarrant County Pest Control offers that small town personal customer service that's hard to find today. When you call Tarrant County Pest Control, the phone is answered by Brent Russell himself and he runs all the jobs himself as well. Each customer gets that personal attention.

Brent Russell is licensed by the Texas Department of Agriculture as a certified applicator and fully insured.
